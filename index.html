Privacy

holid.ai does not store personal data beyond what is needed to run your trip search. We never sell data to third parties.

The datumprikker stores only dates you mark as free. No names, no calendar access, no event titles.

Flight and accommodation searches are powered by affiliate links to Skyscanner, Booking.com, Airbnb, Trainline, and Omio. We earn a small commission if you book, at no extra cost to you.
